TROPICAL WINTER

Temperatures during Rio's tropical winter are warm and pleasant during the day and range from 64°F ​​to 73°F after sunset. It's worth remembering that winter weather in tropical areas changes dramatically with little forecast. Humidity levels are low, with few rainy days. International guests can compare the climate to winter in Miami, Mallorca, and Singapore.

TROPICAL INSPIRATIONS

Tropical inspirations in black tie format help adapt this famous dress code to the climate of Rio de Janeiro and, above all, with plenty of creativity. TROPICALITY offers the opportunity for light fabrics in many colors and endless varieties of shiny and exaggerated accessories. Tonight is the perfect time to bring out rich, exuberant, and highly original ideas. Black and heavy-textured fabrics should be avoided during this style.

BLACK TIE

WOMEN
&
NON-BINARY

For women's black-tie attire, the tradition is a long evening gown, although a sophisticated cocktail dress below the knees in formal fabrics such as silk or velvet is also acceptable. Accessories should be refined, including sophisticated jewelry and a formal evening clutch, with classic heels completing the look. Alternatively, an elegant pantsuit can also be appropriate for black-tie, emphasizing the formal and timeless aesthetic. Original accessories, colorful patterns, unique prints and endless sparkles are welcome and highly appreciated. The main ingredient for a successful dress code is a great mix of creativity, leaving your comfort zone and enjoying this unique moment without limits of expression and individuality.

Avoid: Black fabric, short skirts, short dresses and miniskirts.
Prohibited: sporty shoes and accessories.

IMPORTANT: Step & Repeat will be present at the event entrance with professional lighting for photographic use. We recommend applying makeup for high definition and strong contrast of stage lighting.

BLACK TIE

MEN
&
NON-BINARY

For a men's black tie, wear a matching suit/blazer and pants; a dress shirt and bow tie are essential. Accessories can include cufflinks and a pocket square. Finish the look with polished dress shoes, preferably patent leather or Oxfords. Tropical inspirations are applied to colorful suits, original bow ties, and plenty of creativity with fabrics and textures. Black tie for men at this event doesn't follow the formality of weddings and graduations. There are no style limitations for suits/blazers. Original accessories, colorful patterns, unique prints and endless sparkles are welcome and highly appreciated. The main ingredient for a successful dress code is a great mix of creativity, leaving your comfort zone and enjoying this unique moment without limits of expression and individuality.

Avoid: Black suit/blazer and formal long tie.
Prohibited: Sports shoes and accessories, or those considered smart casual.

IMPORTANT: Step & Repeat will be present at the event entrance with professional lighting for photographic use. We recommend light male makeup (concealer and shine control) for high definition and strong contrast in the stage lighting.

BLACK TIE (CHILDREN UP TO 10 YEARS OLD)

The black tie dress code must also apply to children up to 10 years old, but sports shoes are permitted for greater comfort.
