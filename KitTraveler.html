PLANE TICKETS & SHUTTLES

All international guests must choose Rio de Janeiro/Galeão International Airport (GIG) as their final destination (a 45-minute drive to Ipanema). Travelers planning their first trip to Brazil should make connections in their home country—we do not recommend connections in other Brazilian states or South American countries.

IMPORTANT: We do NOT recommend using public transportation in Rio de Janeiro during your entire stay. ALWAYS use taxis, Uber, and shuttles to travel short and long distances for your comfort and safety.
