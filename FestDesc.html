ABOUT RIO

The city of Rio de Janeiro was chosen for this celebration because it has been Brazil’s most popular destination for centuries. Many of our guests will have the privilege of experiencing Brazil for the first time. Rio represents Brazilian diversity, featuring natural beauty, music, cuisine, culture, and global recognition through famous Hollywood films and international attractions. It draws thousands of spectators to Copacabana Beach each year. Demand for Rio reached unprecedented levels in 2024 and 2025, with the city recording its highest-ever number of foreign visitors. We acknowledge the security challenges Brazil has faced in recent decades, and we have planned everything to ensure a positive experience, including a comprehensive security and comfort plan for all guests.
